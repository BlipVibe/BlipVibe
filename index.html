BlipVibe Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

BlipVibe ("we", "us", "our") operates the BlipVibe social media platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information (provided by you):

  • Email address
  • Username and display name
  • Date of birth (used for age verification)
  • Password (securely hashed; we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Profile bio, avatar photo, and cover photo

Content You Create:

  • Posts, comments, and replies
  • Direct messages
  • Uploaded images
  • Saved posts and folder organization

Usage Data (generated by your activity):

  • Likes, dislikes, and follows
  • Customization preferences (skins, themes, fonts, settings)
  • Coin balance and purchase history
  • Blocked users and reported content

Technical Data:

  • Authentication session tokens (stored in your browser's local storage)
  • Last visited page (stored locally for navigation)

Location Data (only if you opt in):

  • If you enable location sharing in Settings, your device's GPS coordinates are used once to determine your approximate area (city/region). Precise coordinates are not stored in our database. Only the general area name is attached to posts you create while the feature is enabled. Location sharing is off by default.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the BlipVibe platform
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To display your content to other users as intended
  • To deliver direct messages between users
  • To send notifications about activity on your content
  • To enforce our Terms of Use and protect user safety
  • To respond to DMCA takedown requests and legal obligations

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers
  • We do not store your precise GPS coordinates
  • We do not read your direct messages except when required by law or to enforce our Terms

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a cloud infrastructure platform that provides:

  • Encrypted data at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables
  • Secure password hashing via Supabase Auth
  • Cloud hosting with regular backups

Uploaded images (avatars, post photos) are stored in Supabase Storage buckets. These images are publicly accessible by URL to enable display within the platform.

Direct messages are stored in our database and are not end-to-end encrypted. They are protected by access controls that limit visibility to the sender and recipient.

  • Correction: You can edit your profile information, posts, and settings at any time.

  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data by contacting hello@blipvibe.com.
